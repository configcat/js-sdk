Welcome to the ConfigCat + Amplitude A/B Testing Sample app!

The purpose of this sample is to demonstrate how to execute an A/B testing scenario using ConfigCat and Amplitude hand in hand. Here is the original blogpost with more details.

Visitors are split into two groups. 50% of the visitors will see a card with a gray button and the other 50% will see another card with a green button on it. This sample shows a version of the product cards and sends tracking data to Amplitude to analyze visitor behavior.

This is your product card version based on your visitor group. Please click on the Add To Cart button.
The tracking data of the click event will be sent to Amplitude where you can analyze the A/B testing result as described in the blogpost.
