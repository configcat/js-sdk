Welcome to the ConfigCat + Amplitude A/B Testing Sample app!

We would like to show a method of how to A/B testing with ConfigCat and Amplitude. Here is a detailed article.

Let's assume we have a webshop (written in Angular). We would like to make our Add To Cart button on our product card more eye-catching to get visitors to click on it more times and encourage them to buy more products on our site. Weâ€™d like to test two product card variants: a card with a gray button and another one with a green button on it.

This sample shows a version of the product cards and sends tracking data to Amplitude to analyze visitor behavior.
In app.component.ts you can init Amplitude with your API_KEY to be able to review tracking data.

We show the gray button for 50% of the visitors and the green button for the other 50%. We have generated a visitor id for you. This your product card version based on your visitor id. Please click on the Add To Cart button. The tracking data of the click event will be sent to Amplitude where you can analyze the A/B testing result as described in the article.
