ConfigCat A/B Testing Sample app with Amplitude tracking

The purpose of this sample is to demonstrate how to execute an A/B testing scenario using ConfigCat and Amplitude.Here is the original blog post with more details.

Here is the basic concept:

  • Visitors are split into two groups.
  • 50% of the visitors will see a card with a gray button and the other 50% will see another card with a green button on it.
  • This application shows one of the versions of the cards and sends tracking data to Amplitude to analyze visitor behavior.

The card

This is the card version generated for your visitor group. (Hit F5 a few times to get the other version) If you click on the Add To Cart button, the tracking data of the click event will be sent to Amplitude where you can analyze the A/B testing result as described in the blog post.
