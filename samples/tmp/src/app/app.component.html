ConfigCat A/B Testing Sample App with Amplitude tracking

The purpose of this sample is to demonstrate how to implement an A/B testing scenario using ConfigCat. And how to visualize the A/B test results as a funnel using Amplitude. Here is the original blog post with more details.

Here is the basic concept:

  • A "visited" event is sent to Amplitude when the app loads.
  • Visitors are split into two groups.
  • 50% of the visitors will see a card with a gray button and the other 50% will see another card with a green button on it.
  • A "click" event is sent to Amplitude when the visitor clicks the button.
  • A funnel can be set up in Amplitude to visualize how many visitors clicked which version of the button.
